About Jason Olson

Jason Olson is an educator at Gulf Breeze High School dedicated to inspiring curiosity in science and philosophy. Through Physicsy, he shares classroom resources, student projects, and thoughtful explorations at the intersection of physics, biology, AI, and human inquiry.

This site serves as a hub for his honors classes in Physics and Biology, as well as a growing collection of student writing and future AI-assisted philosophy initiatives.

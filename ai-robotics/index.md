# AI & Robotics Blog

This blog chronicles an ongoing experiment: **building an open-source AI student** as preparation for the classroom tools of tomorrow—from intelligent tutoring systems to humanoid platforms like Tesla's Optimus.

As a science teacher, I'm exploring how AI can support learning without replacing the curiosity, struggle, and human connection that make education meaningful. Here you'll find honest reflections on what works, what doesn't, and what we should think carefully about before bringing these tools into schools.

## What You'll Find Here

- **The AI Student project** — Design decisions, architecture, and lessons learned
- **Humanoid robotics** — Experiments with platforms like Berkeley Humanoid Lite
- **Classroom integration** — Practical ideas for using AI responsibly in science education
- **Ethics and philosophy** — Questions worth asking before we automate the classroom
